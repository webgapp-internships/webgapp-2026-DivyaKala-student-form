NAAC

  

As one of the few NAAC accredited (A+ Grade) institutions in the region, Sri Kaliswari College is poised to become one among the premiere educational institutions in the country.
Thanks to the unceasing efforts of the trustees of Sri Kaliswari Trust, and the faculty, the college as a whole, is dedicated to raising the bar of infrastructure and elevating holistic education to international standards.

MISSION

The mission of Sri Kaliswari College is to provide quality higher education that helps students grow academically, professionally, and morally.
Our college aims to develop critical thinking, communication, leadership, and technical skills among students so they can face global challenges with confidence.
We also focuses on value-based education by promoting human values, professional ethics, social responsibility, national integrity, and environmental awareness.
Through inclusive and student-centered learning, the institution strives to empower rural youth and shape them into talented individuals who contribute positively to society and the nation.
